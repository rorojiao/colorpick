Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI color palette generator work?

The palette generator uses color theory algorithms to create harmonious color schemes from your chosen base color. It analyzes hue, saturation, and lightness to generate complementary, analogous, triadic, and split-complementary palettes automatically.

What is the difference between complementary and analogous color schemes?

Complementary colors are opposite each other on the color wheel, creating high contrast. Analogous colors are adjacent on the wheel, creating harmonious, low-contrast palettes. Triadic schemes use three evenly spaced colors for vibrant, balanced designs.

Can I save or export the generated color palette?

You can click any color swatch to copy its HEX value instantly. For a full palette export, copy all HEX codes individually. Each palette URL is shareable so you can send your color selection directly to clients or teammates.
