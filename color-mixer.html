Frequently Asked Questions

How does the color mixer blend two colors?

The color mixer blends two colors using multiple algorithms including RGB linear interpolation, HSL mixing, and perceptual color mixing. You can choose different blend modes to see how the colors interact and pick the result that best suits your design.

What blend modes are available in the color mixer?

The color mixer supports several blend modes including normal RGB mix, HSL blend, LAB perceptual mix, and more. Each mode produces slightly different results, allowing you to find the most visually pleasing or accurate color combination.

Can I see intermediate colors between two mixed colors?

Yes, the color mixer displays a gradient bar showing all intermediate colors between your two chosen colors. You can also generate a series of color steps showing the full transition, making it easy to create multi-step color progressions.
