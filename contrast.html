Frequently Asked Questions

What is the WCAG contrast ratio requirement?

WCAG AA requires a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text. WCAG AAA requires 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text. Higher ratios mean better readability for users with visual impairments.

How do I use the contrast checker?

Select your foreground (text) color and background color using the color pickers or by entering HEX codes. The tool instantly calculates the contrast ratio and shows whether it passes WCAG AA and AAA standards for both normal and large text.

Why is color contrast important for accessibility?

Sufficient color contrast ensures that text is readable for people with low vision, color blindness, or other visual impairments. It is also required by law in many countries under accessibility standards like WCAG 2.1 and ADA compliance guidelines.
