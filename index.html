Frequently Asked Questions

What color tools does ColorPick offer?

ColorPick offers a complete suite of free online color tools: a color picker with HEX/RGB/HSL support, palette generator, WCAG contrast checker, color mixer, shades generator, HEX/RGB/HSL converter, and color blindness simulator.

Is ColorPick free to use?

Yes, all tools on ColorPick are completely free to use with no sign-up or registration required. Simply open any tool and start working with colors instantly in your browser.

Does ColorPick work on mobile devices?

Yes, ColorPick is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. All tools are optimized for touch and mouse input across all screen sizes.
