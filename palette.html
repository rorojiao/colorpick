Frequently Asked Questions

What color scheme types can this palette generator create?

The palette generator supports complementary, analogous, triadic, split-complementary, and monochromatic color schemes. Each scheme is generated automatically from your chosen base color.

How do I copy colors from the generated palette?

Click on any color swatch in the generated palette to copy its HEX code to your clipboard. You can also copy RGB or HSL values by clicking the respective format shown below each swatch.

Can I export the generated color palette?

Yes, you can copy individual HEX codes by clicking any swatch. The palette is also shareable by copying the page URL, which preserves your base color selection for easy sharing with team members.
